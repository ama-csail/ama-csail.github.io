About AMA

AMA is an unfunded project within the Decentralized Information Group at MIT CSAIL. We understand Native Mobile Accessibility to be a large concern for many smartphone users. This project is an effort to organize native mobile accessibility resources to provide an ultimate experience for users of mobile apps.
