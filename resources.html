Accessibility Resources

We've done a lot of research in the space of digital accessibility when planning out the AMA project. We decided to bring all of the resources we found together to share our findings. If you find something to be missing from the resource, please contact accessibility@csail.mit.edu

Our Goals

We hope to transform native mobile development for accessibility by providing resources and tools for developers and designers.

The Team

We are three computer science undergraduate students at MIT with a passion for accessibility and UI design, specifically in the areas of Android and iOS.

Accessibility for All

All parts of this project are open source and available to all developers. We hope to encourage innovation in accessible design for a better world.
